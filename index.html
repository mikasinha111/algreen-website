The Product
☀️

Solar Reflectance — SRI 90+

Reflects the majority of incoming solar radiation. Reduces interior temperatures by an estimated 8–12°C at peak summer, before the heat enters the building.

🌿

Live Algae CO₂ Capture

Cyanobacteria in the formulation actively captures CO₂ from the atmosphere, generating a verifiable, auditable carbon metric per square metre coated.

📊

ESG Dashboard & BRSR Reporting

Real-time CO₂ tracking and energy savings formatted for SEBI BRSR compliance. Turns a maintenance expense into a compliance asset for ESG-reporting clients.

🪣

Drop-In Application

Applied like conventional industrial roof paint — no specialist teams. ~200–250L per 10,000 sq.ft. Annual consumption ~800–1,000L per client per year.
